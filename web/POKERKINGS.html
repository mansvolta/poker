POKER

Poker is the coolest card game of them all, but mostly thought as a gambling or vicious game.

I want to share some thoughts of why I love this game so badly and hopefully It might be of your interest too.

Poker game is consider a game of chance like most of the other games that you can find in casinos, which outcome is just defined by randomness.

Not exactly.

Not true actually. Poker combines chance, strategy, instincts, and player skills.

-Why do you think the same five guys make it to the final table of the World Series of Poker EVERY YEAR? What, are they the luckiest guys in Las Vegas? It’s a skill game Jo!

-Mike McDermott in Rounders 1998

Also, money or gambling does not always have to be involved. You can play with friends or on mobile games that don’t involve money.

So... have fun! No rush on betting or getting in troubles.

Tip: Don’t bet money you don’t have

Tip: good shuffling of the deck is the cornerstone of this game

At the start of each hand of poker, the 52 card deck is shuffled. So no one knows what cards are gonna get nor what cards are going to be in the table.

This is where the magic of poker begins… you never know what’s in the other player’s hand, you have to play with your intuition.

Picture it like this: Every poker game has an arrange of cards that may have never existed, and may never exist again.

There are more possible ways to arrange a deck of cards than there are atoms on earth, so we can be certain that every game is gonna be pretty unique.

In math terms:

8.07x10^67 = 8 followed by 67 Zeros

Texas hold’em modality is the most popular of this game, let’s talk a bit about it:

As many board games, each player starts with the same amount of chips that are used to bet. The goal is to win the pot of chips in each turn, and finally win all the other players chips.

Each player is dealt with two face-down cards.

Then 5 face-up community cards are dealt in 3 rounds: FLOP, TURN and RIVER to the center of the table.

Each player seeks to make the best combination with the 5 community cards and their personal ones.

Chips pot

Tip: protect your cards, don’t let anyone see them

Tip: you can’t trust no one in a poker table

Full of Kings

In each round, players can bet certain amount of their chips, to keep playing, each player have to accept the bet or fold.

If someone bet, and no one pay, the betting player wins the remaining pot.

If others players accept the bet, the one with the top combination of five cards win.

Remember, the best hand doesn’t always win, bluffing is a big deal in poker!

The winner is the one who gets all the chips on the table. This are general rules that you can follow to have

a friendly

game.

You’ll need your own unique poker face...

Some hide over a hoodie or sunglasses, some change their look, some play just casual… make your choice. It’s kind of a Fight Club thing, even if you know the person that you’re playing with, is not the same person you know in real life.

Personality counts, is not the smartest or the craziest who wins, you have to play your own way, prevail among the others, be certain and aggressive, control your opponents and most important... yourself.

The first time I was in a poker table I felt overwhelmed by the energy surrounding each player, The hardest thing is to avoid anyone getting into your head. So good luck!
